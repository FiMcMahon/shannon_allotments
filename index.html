Who are we?

Shannon Allotments is a small public group of allotments located in Shannon, Co.Clare, Ireland. Located near to the banks of the Shannon Estuary, we are a group of about 40 allotment holders growing our own vegetables, fruit and herbs. The allotments are allocated via Clare County Council. To add your name to the waiting list to rent an allotment, an application form can be found on Clare County Council website.
